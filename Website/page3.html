MacBook Air

The MacBook Air is a popular laptop in Apple's Mac lineup, known for its lightweight design and portability. It features a high-resolution Retina display, Touch ID for secure access, and Apple's M1 chip for fast and efficient performance. With up to 18 hours of battery life, the MacBook Air is ideal for users who need a computer that can last all day. The laptop also includes a range of features such as a backlit keyboard, Force Touch trackpad, and Thunderbolt 3 ports. Its sleek and thin design makes it easy to carry around, making it a popular choice for students, professionals, and anyone on-the-go. Overall, the MacBook Air is a reliable and versatile laptop that delivers a smooth and efficient computing experience.

Name MacBook Air
Colors Gold, Silver, Space Gray
Storage 256GB, 512GB
Price From €1,129

MacBook Pro

The MacBook Pro is a line of high-performance laptops designed and manufactured by Apple Inc. It is a popular choice among professionals who require powerful hardware for demanding tasks such as video editing, graphic design, and programming. The latest models feature Apple's M1 chip, which delivers faster performance, longer battery life, and better efficiency than previous generations. The MacBook Pro also includes advanced features such as the Touch Bar, a customizable OLED strip that replaces the function keys and provides contextual shortcuts for various applications. Additionally, the MacBook Pro features a Retina display with True Tone technology, which automatically adjusts the color temperature based on the ambient lighting for a more natural viewing experience. Overall, the MacBook Pro is a premium laptop that offers powerful hardware, advanced features, and a sleek design for professionals and power users.

Name MacBook Pro
Colors Silver, Space Gray
Storage 256GB, 512GB, 1TB, 2TB, 4TB
Price From €1,449

iMac

The iMac is a line of all-in-one desktop computers designed and produced by Apple Inc. The iMac features a slim design that integrates the computer and display into one unit, making it a space-saving solution for those looking for a desktop computer. The latest iMac models are powered by the Apple M1 chip and feature high-resolution displays, advanced cameras, and powerful speakers, making them ideal for video conferencing, content creation, and entertainment. The iMac is also known for its ease of use and intuitive operating system, macOS, which offers a seamless experience for users of all skill levels. Overall, the iMac is a reliable and powerful computer that has become a staple in the Apple lineup.

Name iMac
Colors Blue, Green, Pink, Silver, Yellow, Orange, Purple.
Storage 256GB, 512GB, 1TB, 2TB
Price From €1,449

iMac Pro

The iMac Pro is a professional-grade all-in-one desktop computer developed and produced by Apple Inc. It was released in December 2017 and is designed for professionals who require a high-performance computer for demanding tasks such as video editing, 3D rendering, and scientific simulations. The iMac Pro features a 27-inch Retina 5K display, a powerful Intel Xeon processor with up to 18 cores, up to 256GB of RAM, and up to 4TB of SSD storage. It also includes a Radeon Pro Vega graphics card, which provides excellent graphics performance for demanding applications. Additionally, it features a built-in 1080p FaceTime HD camera, a high-quality audio system, and a range of connectivity options. The iMac Pro is a highly capable and versatile computer that is well-suited to the needs of professionals in a range of industries.

Name iMac Pro
Colors Blue, Green, Pink, Silver, Yellow, Orange, Purple.
Storage 1TB, 2TB, 4TB
Price From €5,449
